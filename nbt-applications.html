FAQs

The NBT is a standardized test that assesses academic literacy, quantitative literacy, and mathematics skills for university readiness in South Africa. Learn more at NBT Website.

Most universities in South Africa require students, including matriculants and mature students, to take the NBT for admission. Check requirements at NBT Website.

Registrations are made on the NBT website. Click on book a test and follow the prompts to complete the process. We'll do it for you!

The NBT consists of two tests: Academic Literacy and Quantitative Literacy (AQL) and Mathematics (MAT). The AQL test is required for all programs, while the MAT test is required for programs that require mathematics. We can advise you!

The costs for the NBT are:
AQL Test: R185
AQL and MAT Tests: R370
Re-mark: R250
